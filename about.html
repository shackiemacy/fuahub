About Fua Hub

Revolutionizing household services by connecting trusted professionals with busy homeowners across Kenya.

Our Mission

Fua Hub was founded with a mission to simplify your life by revolutionizing household services. We believe everyone deserves a clean, comfortable home without the stress of managing household chores.

Our platform connects busy individuals and families with trusted, professional service providers who deliver quality household services right to your doorstep. From laundry and cleaning to cooking and maintenance, we make it easy to get help when you need it.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a team of entrepreneurs who understood the daily struggle of balancing work and household responsibilities, Fua Hub emerged from a simple idea: what if getting help with household tasks was as easy as ordering food online?

Starting in Nairobi, we've grown to serve thousands of families across Kenya, connecting them with skilled professionals who take pride in their work.
